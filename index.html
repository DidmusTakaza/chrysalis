Discover Hope and Healing


At Chrysalis, we believe in the transformative power of faith and recovery. Our Christian-centered rehabilitation centre is dedicated to helping individuals overcome all forms of addiction, including substance abuse, behavioral addictions, and co-occurring mental health disorders. We recognize that addiction is not only a physical challenge but also a spiritual journey, and we are committed to addressing the whole person—body, mind, and spirit, with core-emphasis on relapse prevention

What is Our Success Rate?

Success rate is difficult to measure. It depends on the motivation of the client. Staying clean and sober in rehab is not difficult; the hard work will start once the client is leaving treatment and has to face the outside world with all its triggers. One thing we can guarantee is that we will give our clients all the tools to be successful once going home and provide them with the right aftercare. The therapies we use are used by our overseas partners who have achieved success rates of over 90%.

Aftercare Services

Chrysalis’ Discharge Planner begins aftercare planning as soon as a client is admitted. This planning phase is unique to each client and can include identifying and scheduling appointments with mental and behavioral health therapists and medical providers (psychiatrists or other addiction medicine doctors). Aftercare planning includes securing sobriety-supportive living arrangements when necessary, discovering local 12 Step chapter meetings, and gathering any necessary materials when a client is seeking employment or educational opportunities after discharge.

At discharge, Alumni are automatically enrolled in our “Flourish for Life” Alumni Program; access to the Alumni Coordinator is immediate, and there will be at least one Alumni support activity or associated platform within a week of discharge.

Demand Reduction Service

Chrysalis has helped thousands of young adults and teens overcome mental health, behavioral, and substance use disorders by transforming these challenges into opportunities for personal growth, and by helping them build lives worth protecting. We identify at-risk youths and provide training and therapies to help deal with challenges that may degenerate into substance use disorder and poor mental health issues.

For three years, we have been positively affecting the lives of our clients and their families through a comprehensive three-phased approach designed to help young people take the learnings from therapy and successfully apply them in the real world. Learn more about our effective combination of clinical best practices, academic and vocational programming, life skills development, and recreational experience by selecting one of our age-specific programs above.

Phased programming is integral to Chrysalis and is among its earliest and most important treatment innovations. Segmenting Chrysalis extended care programming into three phases transforms its efficacy and benefits for each client and family while maintaining a healthy and growth-oriented milieu that enhances the treatment experience. Our flagship program for adolescents, the Pachitubu BenaBau project, has proved immensely successful in reducing demand in teens and adolescents.

Invite the Chrysalis demand reduction team to discuss how this may be successfully rolled out in your school or community.
