Psychological Services

Our psychological services aim to provide comprehensive support for individuals facing mental health challenges. We specialize in various psychological disorders, offering evidence-based treatments to help clients regain control over their lives.

Common psychological conditions we treat include:

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

We offer specialized treatment for OCD, utilizing Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and exposure response prevention to help clients manage their symptoms effectively.

Schizophrenia

Our schizophrenia treatment program focuses on medication management and psychosocial support to help individuals lead fulfilling lives while managing their symptoms.

Anxiety Disorders

We provide comprehensive treatment for anxiety disorders, including Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Panic Disorder, and Social Anxiety, using a combination of therapy and medication as needed.

Depression

Our depression treatment combines psychotherapy and medication management, tailored to individual needs, to promote healing and recovery.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

We specialize in treating PTSD through trauma-informed care, helping clients process their experiences and develop effective coping strategies.
