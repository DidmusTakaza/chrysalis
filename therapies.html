What We Offer

Different Types of Therapy

At Chrysalis, we offer a variety of therapeutic options to cater to the unique needs of each individual. Our approaches are designed to foster healing, growth, and recovery.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors, helping individuals develop coping strategies.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

DBT combines cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness strategies, beneficial for emotional regulation and interpersonal effectiveness.

Family Therapy

Family therapy involves working with families to improve communication and resolve conflicts, fostering understanding and support. We believe in family being important

Group Therapy

Group therapy provides a supportive environment for individuals to share experiences and learn from each other, enhancing recovery and a good weel being

Art Therapy

Art therapy utilizes creative processes to help individuals express emotions and explore their feelings in a therapeutic setting.

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

This approach emphasizes mindfulness practices to enhance awareness and acceptance of the present moment, reducing stress and anxiety.

Trauma-Informed Therapy

This therapy recognizes the impact of trauma on individuals and incorporates strategies to promote healing and resilience for a better wellbeing

Holistic Therapy

Holistic therapy addresses the whole person, integrating physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects to promote overall wellness.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors. It helps individuals develop coping strategies for a variety of issues, including anxiety, depression, and stress management.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

DBT combines cognitive-behavioral techniques with mindfulness strategies. It is particularly beneficial for individuals struggling with emotional regulation and interpersonal effectiveness.

Family Therapy

Family therapy focuses on improving communication and resolving conflicts within families. It helps families understand each other better and supports healthier relationships.

Group Therapy

Group therapy offers a supportive environment where individuals can share their experiences and learn from one another, facilitating personal growth and recovery.

Art Therapy

Art therapy utilizes creative processes to help individuals express their emotions and explore their feelings in a supportive therapeutic environment.

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

This approach emphasizes mindfulness practices to enhance awareness and acceptance of the present moment, helping reduce stress and anxiety.

Trauma-Informed Therapy

This therapy recognizes the impact of trauma on individuals and incorporates strategies to promote healing, resilience, and recovery.

Holistic Therapy

Holistic therapy addresses the whole person, integrating physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects to promote overall wellness and personal growth.
